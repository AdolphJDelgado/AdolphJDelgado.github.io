About

A researcher, teacher, and strategist with a community-first lens.

My work connects public health, applied statistics, generative AI, and real-world implementation.

I specialize in building practical solutions for organizations that need more than “data.” They need clarity. They need strategy. They need evidence that can be explained to funders, partners, leadership, and communities.
